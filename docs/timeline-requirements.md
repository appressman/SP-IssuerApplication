# Reg CF Launch Timeline Requirements

*Extracted from: Reg-CF Issuer Launch Task List & Timeline Template (60 days)*

This document maps the actual launch timeline requirements to issuer interview questions.

---

## Timeline Overview

| Phase | Hours | Key Deadline |
|-------|-------|--------------|
| Setup | 2.4 hrs | Day 1 |
| Campaign Communications | 36.75 hrs | Weeks 1-4 |
| Building Portal Page | 61.25 hrs | Weeks 1-3 |
| Test The Waters (Optional) | 2.5 hrs | Week 2+ |
| Campaign Compliance | 144.75 hrs | Weeks 3-8 |
| Managing Live Offering | 4.85 hrs | 90-day campaign |
| Post-Raise Obligations | 2.75 hrs | Post-close |
| **Total** | **~255 hours** | **~6 months** |

---

## Phase-by-Phase Issuer Requirements

### Phase 0: Setup (2.4 hours)

**Tasks:**
- S.1 Portal Listing Agreement
- S.2 Invoice/Payment
- S.3 Data Room Access
- S.4 Kickoff Questionnaire
- S.5 Zoom Kickoff Meeting

**Interview Questions to Add:**
- "Are you prepared to sign a portal listing agreement to begin?"
- "Who from your team will be the primary point of contact for the campaign?"
- "Can you commit to a kickoff meeting within the first week?"

---

### Phase 1: Campaign Communications (36.75 hours)

**Tasks:**
- C.1 Assign Communications Manager
- C.2 Outreach Goals & Strategy
- C.3 Campaign Tools Setup
- C.4 CrowdBuilder Setup (Zoom)
- C.5 Investor Incentives & Sources
- C.6 Campaign Content Calendar
- C.7 UTM Tags
- C.8 Personal Outreach
- C.9 Email Campaigns
- C.10 Campaign Content (20 hours!)
- C.11 CRM Setup
- C.12 Build Email Campaigns

**Interview Questions to Add:**
- "Who will manage investor communications during your campaign?"
- "Do you have an existing email list or customer database you can reach out to?"
- "What investor incentives or perks are you considering offering?"
- "Can you commit approximately 20 hours to creating campaign content (videos, images, copy)?"
- "Do you have existing marketing assets (videos, professional photos, testimonials)?"

---

### Phase 2: Building Portal Page (61.25 hours)

**Tasks:**
- P.1 Assign Portal Manager
- P.2 Review Sample Campaign Page
- P.3 Submit Domain & Theme Assets (logos, colors, brand guidelines)
- P.4 Draft Campaign Page Content (3 hours)
- P.5 Submit Portal Page Media
- P.6 Submit Management Settings
- P.7 Portal Page Build Out (48 hours - done by platform)
- P.8-P.16 Reviews, edits, tracking, analytics

**Interview Questions to Add:**
- "Do you have brand assets ready (logo, colors, fonts, brand guidelines)?"
- "Do you have high-quality photos or video of your team, product, or operations?"
- "Do you have customer testimonials or case studies to feature?"
- "Are you comfortable writing or editing marketing copy for your campaign page?"
- "Do you have access to your domain's DNS settings for custom subdomain setup?"

---

### Phase 3: Campaign Compliance (144.75 hours)

**Tasks:**
- L.0 Assign Attorney (3 hours to find if not already engaged)
- L.1 Assign CPA (1 hour)
- L.2 Submit Owner & Officer Information
- L.3 Submit Bank Account Application
- L.4 Submit Basic Business Forms
- L.5 Bad Actor Check
- L.6 Establish Bank Account (escrow)
- L.7 Submit Business Plan/Pitch Deck (4 hours)
- L.8 Submit Financial Statements (4 hours)
- L.9 Submit EDGAR Form ID (1 hour)
- L.10 Draft State Notice Filing
- L.11 Confirm Intermediary Information
- L.12 Subscription Agreement Requests (1.5 hours)
- L.13 Tigermark Policy Confirmation
- L.14 Service Contracts
- L.15 **Form C Draft (120 hours!)** - This is the bulk of the work
- L.16 Form C Draft Review
- L.17-L.20 Filing and launch

**Interview Questions to Add:**
- "Are all officers and directors prepared to provide full background information?"
- "Can your company open a dedicated bank account for the offering proceeds?"
- "Do you have an EDGAR account (CIK number)? Have you filed with the SEC before?"
- "Are you aware that preparing the Form C disclosure document takes approximately 120 hours of combined effort?"
- "Can you provide complete financial statements within 2-3 weeks of kickoff?"

---

### Phase 4: Managing Live Offering (4.85 hours + ongoing)

**Tasks:**
- M.0 Assign Campaign Monitoring
- M.1 Launch Day Testing
- M.2 Campaign Rules Reminders
- M.3 Connect Disbursement Account (21 days post-launch)
- M.4 Monitor Offering Activity (ongoing - 90 days)
- M.5 Respond to Forum Posts
- M.6 Nudge Investors
- M.7 Send Campaign Updates
- M.8 Request Funds Disbursement
- M.9 Live Offering Changes Guidance
- M.10 Close Offering

**Interview Questions to Add:**
- "Who will monitor and respond to investor questions during the 90-day campaign?"
- "Can you commit to regular campaign updates (at least bi-weekly)?"
- "Do you understand that you cannot make certain types of promotional claims during the offering?"

---

### Phase 5: Post-Raise Obligations (2.75 hours + ongoing)

**Tasks:**
- O.0 Assign Investor Relations Manager
- O.1 Notify Investors of IR Contact
- O.2 Form C-U Final Update Notice (5 days post-close)
- O.3 Investor Perks Fulfillment (30 days post-close)
- O.4 Deliver Investor Tax Documents
- O.5 Reg-CF Annual Reporting
- O.6 Termination of Reporting

**Interview Questions to Add:**
- "Do you understand the ongoing annual reporting requirements (Form C-AR)?"
- "Who will handle investor relations and communications after the raise?"
- "Are you prepared to issue tax documents (K-1s, 1099s) to all investors?"
- "If offering perks, can you fulfill them within 30 days of closing?"

## Time Commitment Summary

### For Issuer Team

| Activity | Hours | Timeline |
|----------|-------|----------|
| Setup & Kickoff | 3-5 | Week 1 |
| Content Creation | 20-30 | Weeks 1-4 |
| Compliance Documents | 10-15 | Weeks 2-6 |
| Form C Input/Review | 15-25 | Weeks 4-8 |
| Campaign Management | 20-40 | 90-day campaign |
| Post-Raise | 5-10 | Ongoing |
| **Total Issuer Time** | **75-125 hours** | **6+ months** |

### Questions About Capacity

- "Can you or a team member dedicate 10-15 hours per week to this campaign for the next 2 months?"
- "Who on your team will be responsible for providing requested documents and information?"
- "Are there any upcoming events (travel, product launches, busy seasons) that might conflict with this timeline?"

## Red Flags from Timeline Perspective

Flag these issues for further discussion:

1. **No dedicated person** for communications or investor relations
2. **Unrealistic launch date** (wants to launch in < 30 days)
3. **No existing content** or assets (adds significant time)
4. **CPA not engaged** and financials not prepared (can add 4+ weeks)
5. **Attorney not engaged** (can add 2+ weeks)
6. **Multiple owners** with complex backgrounds (bad actor checks take time)
7. **No email list** or investor network (marketing challenge)
8. **Unfamiliar with ongoing obligations** (may not be ready for compliance burden)
